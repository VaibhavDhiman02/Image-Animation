ELEPHANT

A majestic giant with a gentle soul, the elephant roams the savannah, embodying strength and grace. they are also known as Gentle giant of the wild.

RABBIT

A small, nimble creature, the rabbit hops through meadows with grace and speed, agility and curiosity. Its soft fur and tcute nose add to its charm.

LION

The lion, king of the savannah, exudes power and majesty with its imposing presence and regal mane. As a fierce predator and a symbol of strength.

GIRRAFE

The giraffe, with its height and long neck, gracefully walks across the plains. it reaches the treetops with ease, elegance and serenity in the wild.

HIPPOPOTAMOUS

The hippopotamus, a massive and powerful creature, spends much of its time submerged in rivers and lakes. It moves with surprising agility in water.
